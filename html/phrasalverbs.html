Uncle Bob goes Flat Hunting

He spent the weekend looking through the newspapers trying to find something in his price range. He decided he needed some advice so he looked up my phone number in his address book and gave me a ring. The next day we spent the afternoon looking at some possibilities on the outskirts of San Sebastian, in a suburb called Renteria. He was looking for a bargain but I told him to look out for flats which needed a lot of work. doing. In every flat we looked over the electrics and the plumbing. All the flats we saw were quite pricey so we looked into the possibility of getting a mortgage from the bank.
